ThinkMate Privacy Policy

Developer: Lumalien (lumalien.com) · Effective June 9, 2026

ThinkMate records meetings, transcribes them, and produces a short summary. This policy explains what data the app handles and where it goes. In short: your meetings live on your device, and the only time audio leaves your device is to be transcribed and summarized — it is never stored on our servers.

What we handle

  • Audio recordings. When you tap record, ThinkMate captures audio using your device's microphone.
  • Meeting metadata. A title containing the date and time the recording was made.
  • Generated results. The transcript and summary produced from your recording.

We do not require an account. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics.

How your recording is processed

To turn audio into a transcript and summary, the app sends your recording over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to the ThinkMate processing service. That service is stateless: it processes your audio in memory, returns the result, and deletes the temporary file immediately. It does not keep your audio, transcript, or summary.

To perform the processing, the service uses two third-party providers:

  • Deepgram — converts speech to text. See the Deepgram Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — generates the summary. See the Anthropic Privacy Policy.

Your audio and transcript are shared with these providers solely to perform transcription and summarization for you, and only for as long as needed to return the result.

Where your data is stored

Your transcripts and summaries are saved only on your device, in the app's local storage. We have no server-side copy and no ability to access your saved meetings. If you use the app on the web, history is held only in your browser session.

Your control and deletion

  • Delete any single meeting from the History screen.
  • Use “Clear” in History to permanently delete all saved meetings on the device.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children

ThinkMate is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Security

Audio is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Saved results remain on your device under the operating system's app sandbox.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
